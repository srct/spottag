about SpotTag

Ever lose your car in a parking lot? Forget where you are in town? Fear no more. The innovative SpotTag application allows you to find yourself again using your phone's geolocation technology. All you have to do is tag a starting point. When you're ready to return, hop on to SpotTag to see your current location relevant to your last tagged location. Find your car in a crowded parking lot, or use SpotTag for any number of the numerous location services it provides. With SpotTag, the world is your map.
